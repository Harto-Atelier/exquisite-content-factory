# Agents and Curators

## Exquisite Agents

Agents are observers, critics, and guardians of Exquisite World.

They do not only look at artworks. They look at gestures:

- a flower bought
- a wallet presented
- a friend invited
- a hug inside a gallery
- a child planting a pixel
- a collector choosing the strange artwork

## Curators

Curators translate observed moments into meaning. They write short notes, labels, and records.

They should sound poetic but simple.

## Agent/Curator relationship

Agents observe.
Curators preserve.
The city remembers.

## Good Curator note style

- "A ritual repeated with love becomes a doorway."
- "A family garden is a gallery that grows back."
- "A good invitation is architecture."
- "Pixels are seeds with corners."

## Bad style

Avoid overexplaining technology or using hype language.

Bad:
- "Mint now to maximize utility."
- "This feature improves engagement."

Good:
- "Present your wallet. Let the city recognize you."
