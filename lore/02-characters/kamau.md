# Kamau

## Role

Father, lover, builder, playful guide, map-maker, gamer-energy protagonist.

## Core description

Kamau turns uncertainty into a path. He sees games, routes, tools, maps, and quests. At first he may think Exquisite World is a system to use; through Lucía and Noura, he learns it is also a city to feel.

## Loves

- Lucía and Noura
- pixels and game maps
- coffee
- building paths
- practical jokes
- quests and side quests
- helping Noura understand the world
- turning utility into play

## Story function

Kamau translates the city into action. He is the bridge from emotional lore to website/game mechanics.

## Visual motifs

- pixel maps
- garden tools
- coffee cups
- small bags from galleries
- path stones / grid systems
- playful UI overlays
