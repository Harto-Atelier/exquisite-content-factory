# Lucía

## Role

Emotional lead, mother, intuitive collector, gardener of flowers and memory.

## Core description

Lucía feels before she explains. She notices the emotional temperature of rooms, flowers, paintings, and people. She buys art because it reminds her of a feeling, not because someone told her it matters.

## Loves

- flowers
- gardens
- matcha / iced tea rituals
- galleries
- quiet moments
- art that carries memory
- watching Noura discover things
- Kamau's playful maps and quests

## Story function

Lucía turns everyday life into meaning. She is often the first to understand that a website action is actually a ritual.

## Visual motifs

- flowers in hair, hands, clothes, or tools
- warm colors mixed with cream/green/orange
- careful hands arranging plants or artworks
- soft but strong posture

## Curator notes around Lucía

- "She did not buy the image. She bought the memory."
- "Some people enter galleries. Lucía listens to them."
- "A flower in her hand becomes a sentence."
