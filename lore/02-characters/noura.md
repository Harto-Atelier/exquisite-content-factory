# Noura

## Role

Lucía and Kamau's daughter. Child protagonist. The one who sees hidden beauty first.

## Core description

Noura is curious, playful, and visually intelligent. She loves flowers and pixels because both can grow into something larger than they look. She often touches the wrong pixel and opens the right door.

## Important canon correction

Noura is their kid, not an animal companion.

## Loves

- pixel flowers
- gardens
- secret doors
- tiny artworks
- games
- holding both parents' hands
- planting things
- asking impossible questions

## Story function

Noura is the wonder engine. She reveals what the adults miss. She makes the world viral, tender, and magical without needing to explain it.

## Visual motifs

- small hands planting glowing pixel seeds
- bright eyes
- flower drawings
- tiny garden tools
- pixel toys
- child-sized museum labels

## Curator notes around Noura

- "Children find doors because nobody told them walls are final."
- "Noura planted a pixel and waited for it to bloom."
- "The city always noticed her first."
