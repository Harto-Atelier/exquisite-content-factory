# Location Registry

## Museum Plaza
Arrival point. Public square. First glimpse of the city.

## Gallery District
Small galleries, first artworks, family purchases, evening windows.

## Café of Critics
Matcha, coffee, iced tea, family rituals, soft conversations, hidden paintings.

## Pixel Garden
The family garden. Flowers and pixels grow together. Noura plants glowing pixel seeds.

## Flower Market
Stalls of flowers, pixel seeds, garden tools, art editions, gifts, invitations.

## Claim Kiosk
Website claim flow as a city ritual. The place where the city recognizes a wallet.

## Leaderboard Tower
Ranking and points visualized as a tower/garden that grows through participation.

## Roulette Fountain
Daily roulette visualized as a public fountain/ceremony where the city notices someone.

## Wallet Gallery
A private room opened by a wallet. Contains artworks, gestures, invitations, flowers, memories.

## Ordinals Archive
The deep archive: parent, children, canonical records, inscriptions, history.

## Burn Garden
The place where loss becomes memory, soil, and new growth.

## Trading Desk
TRAC bridge and proposal mechanics transformed into a cultural exchange desk.
