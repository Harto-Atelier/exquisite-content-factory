# Voice and Tone Guide

## North star

Simple enough for a child. Deep enough for a curator.

## Good tone

- emotional
- concise
- poetic but clear
- warm
- art-world but not pretentious
- magical but grounded in family rituals

## Avoid

- NFT hype
- corporate dashboard copy
- generic AI fantasy
- overexplaining mechanics
- direct imitation of Pixar/Disney IP or character design

## Sentence patterns

- "Your wallet is not a wallet. It is a gallery."
- "Pixels are seeds with corners."
- "A family garden is a gallery that grows back."
- "Present your wallet. Let the city recognize you."
- "Art begins in life."

## Character voices

Lucía: intuitive, warm, poetic.
Kamau: playful, practical, quest/game metaphors.
Noura: curious, direct, wonder-first.
Agents: observant, quiet, precise.
Curators: short poetic notes.
