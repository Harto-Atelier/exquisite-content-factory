# Visual Language Guide

## Core style

Exquisite World has two complementary visual lanes:

1. Rich world scenes: colorful semi-pixel / voxel / semi-real art city.
2. Minimal emotional scenes: clean PFP-inspired backgrounds with simple compositions, radial gradients, flowers, pixels, characters, and very little noise.

The system should move between both. Some videos can be detailed voxel/pixel worlds; others should be minimal, graphic, and calm. Less is more.

## Visual ingredients for rich world scenes

- flowers
- pixels
- gardens
- galleries
- cafés
- museum labels
- warm light
- black / cream / grey / orange UI signage
- framed artworks
- small city plazas
- game-like paths and maps

## Visual ingredients for minimal scenes

- one character or family silhouette/pose
- one flower, one pixel seed, one artwork, or one UI object
- soft radial gradient background
- pink background with light orange-to-pink radial glow
- blue background with light blue-to-blue radial glow
- PFP-like composition and negative space
- simple caption or Curator note
- no clutter, no over-rendering

## Important direction

Exquisite World should feel like a living art city, not only a dark archive or terminal.

It can borrow emotional clarity from animated family films, but it must not copy protected characters, styles, or brand identities.

## Family scenes

Lucía, Kamau, and Noura should often be doing simple things:

- gardening
- choosing art
- drinking coffee/matcha/iced tea
- walking through galleries
- playing games
- planting pixel flowers
- opening wallet rooms
- inviting friends
- hugging
- discovering hidden doors

## Prompt base — rich world scene

Lucía, Kamau, and their daughter Noura inside Exquisite World, a colorful semi-pixel / voxel cultural city of flowers, galleries, cafés, pixel gardens, museum labels, warm light, black orange cream UI signage, emotional family story, premium art-world fantasy, cinematic vertical 9:16.

## Prompt base — minimal PFP-style scene

Minimal Exquisite World composition, Lucía, Kamau, and Noura as a small family moment, one symbolic flower or pixel seed, clean radial gradient background, pink with soft light orange-to-pink glow or light blue-to-blue glow, lots of negative space, premium PFP background aesthetic, simple emotional pose, subtle pixel details, no clutter, less is more, vertical 9:16.

## When to use each lane

Use rich world scenes for:
- discovery
- city locations
- Claim Kiosk / Leaderboard Tower / Ordinals Archive
- garden quests
- establishing shots
- worldbuilding episodes

Use minimal scenes for:
- emotional quotes
- Curator notes
- love/family micro-moments
- fast daily shorts
- PFP-compatible visual identity
- videos where the caption/voice is the main focus

## Edit rhythm

Do not make every video visually dense. A strong daily feed should alternate:

- detailed voxel/pixel world
- minimal gradient emotional frame
- screen/website utility
- character close-up
- flower/pixel symbolic shot
