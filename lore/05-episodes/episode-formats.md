# Episode Formats

## Core 30-second shape

1. Hook: relatable human moment.
2. Family action: Lucía, Kamau, or Noura doing something simple.
3. Wonder beat: flowers/pixels/art/website ritual responds.
4. Curator note: one poetic sentence.
5. CTA or emotional exit.

## Repeatable series

### Love, Curated
Lucía and Kamau love stories. Art interprets ordinary romance.

### Noura's Pixel Seeds
Noura plants, touches, or notices a pixel that opens a room.

### The Pixel Garden
Family gardening stories where flowers and pixels grow together.

### Café of Critics
Coffee, matcha, iced tea, family rituals, hidden paintings, soft moments.

### Wallet Gallery
A wallet opens rooms of family, taste, memory, and collection.

### Garden Quests
Kamau turns tasks into game quests for Noura.

### Agent Observations
Agents observe ordinary gestures and Curators write notes.

### Website Rituals
Utility stories for claim, leaderboard, roulette, referrals, ordinals, trading, burns.

## Season 1: The First Garden

1. Lucía and Kamau arrive looking for one artwork.
2. Noura finds a pixel seed.
3. The wallet opens a room.
4. The first flower grows from a screen.
5. Kamau maps the Gallery District.
6. Lucía buys a painting without knowing why.
7. Noura opens the Claim Kiosk.
8. The first Agent observes them.
9. The family plants the Pixel Garden.
10. The city recognizes them.
