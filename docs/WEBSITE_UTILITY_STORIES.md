# Website Utility as Story Rituals

## Connect wallet

Plain: Connect your wallet.
Story: Present your wallet at the city gate.

Lucía/Kamau/Noura version:
Kamau thinks he is logging into a website. Lucía sees a city. Noura pecks the gate. The wallet opens into a gallery.

## Claim Center

Plain: Go to claim and collect.
Story: Visit the Claim Kiosk. Let the city recognize you.

## Leaderboard

Plain: Check your points and rank.
Story: Climb the Leaderboard Tower. See where your gallery stands.

## Daily roulette

Plain: Daily chance/reward.
Story: The Roulette Fountain chooses who the city notices today.

## Referrals

Plain: Invite friends.
Story: Invite someone into the room. A good invitation is architecture.

## Discord boost / verification

Plain: Verify tier and claim.
Story: Show your badge at the gallery door; the city records your support.

## TRAC bridge / trading

Plain: Trading/bridge workflow.
Story: Visit the Trading Desk. Propose the exchange. Let the archive witness.

## Ordinals archive

Plain: Parent/children/canonical inscriptions.
Story: Enter the Ordinals Archive, where every child has a wall and every duplicate has a shadow.

## Burn Garden

Plain: Burn/duplicates/canonical supply.
Story: Walk through the Burn Garden. Nothing disappears; it becomes history.
