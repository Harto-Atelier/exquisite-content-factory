# Exquisite World Bible

## Premise

Exquisite World is a colorful semi-pixel / voxel cultural city where art, love, wallets, flowers, and on-chain rituals become part of the same living gallery.

Lucía and Kamau are in love. They travel through this city with Noura, a tiny chicken companion who somehow understands art, rituals, and hidden website flows better than everyone else.

The Agents observe. The Curators interpret. The website unlocks rooms in the story.

## Main characters

### Lucía

Role: emotional lead, intuitive collector, person who feels meaning before she can explain it.

Traits:
- curious, romantic, observant
- notices tiny details: flowers, colors, frames, cups, silence
- buys art because it reminds her of memory, love, or identity
- often understands the city before Kamau does

Possible lines:
- "This room feels like it remembers us."
- "I don't know why, but this one is ours."
- "It is not a website. It is a city."

### Kamau

Role: warm playful lead, protector, builder, gamer/quest energy.

Traits:
- loyal, funny, grounded
- tries to make Lucía laugh
- sees maps, quests, progress, levels
- begins practical but becomes more poetic through Lucía

Possible lines:
- "Are we lost, or is this a side quest?"
- "Inventory empty. Taste loading."
- "Noura, please stop pecking the archive."

### Noura

Role: tiny chicken companion, mascot, comic relief, mystical taste compass.

Traits:
- small, expressive, serious for a chicken
- pecks buttons, discovers hidden rooms, chooses weird artworks
- falls asleep during curator speeches
- may secretly be the best curator

Recurring jokes:
- Noura always chooses the best artwork.
- Noura pecks the correct website button.
- Noura is somehow on the leaderboard.
- Noura has a wallet.
- Noura is banned from three galleries but invited to every opening.
- Noura only respects one Agent.

## World locations

- Museum Plaza — arrival point and public square.
- Gallery District — cozy galleries, openings, first artworks.
- Café of Critics — matcha, coffee, iced tea, conversations, rituals.
- Flower Market — gifts, daily participation, La Floristería energy.
- Claim Kiosk — website claim center as a city ritual.
- Leaderboard Tower — rankings as a growing garden/tower.
- Roulette Fountain — daily roulette as a public ceremony.
- Ordinals Archive — parent/children/canonical supply/burn lore.
- Burn Garden — transformation, memory, duplicates, loss becoming history.
- Trading Desk — TRAC bridge / market / proposed trades.
- Wallet Gallery — each connected wallet becomes a room.

## Visual language

- colorful semi-pixel / voxel / semi-real 3D
- warm gallery lighting
- flowers everywhere
- art frames, vitrines, cartelas, agent cards, museum labels
- black / cream / grey / orange UI as signage and overlay, not the whole world
- cozy but premium: Animal Crossing warmth + contemporary art gallery + on-chain bureaucracy

## Tone

Emotional, concise, strange, art-world poetic, never generic NFT hype.

Good:
- "Your wallet is not a wallet. It is a gallery."
- "Present your wallet."
- "A ritual repeated with love becomes a doorway."
- "Taste often arrives wearing feathers."
- "A good invitation is architecture."

Avoid:
- "gm wagmi mint now"
- corporate SaaS tutorial voice
- explaining mechanics before the world feels alive
- directly imitating Disney/Pixar visual IP

## Creative formula

Someone wanted X.
They found Y.
Noura accidentally did Z.
The Curator wrote: "..."
The website/world action becomes a ritual.

Example:

Lucía wanted coffee. Kamau remembered the order. Noura pecked the café wall and opened a hidden painting. The Curator wrote: "A ritual repeated with love becomes a doorway." Present your wallet.
